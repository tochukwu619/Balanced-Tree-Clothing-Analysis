This report presents a structured analysis of sales performance covering high-level metrics, transaction behaviour, and product-level insights.

## 4. Summary & Recommendations

1. **Shirt and Jacket segments drive the most revenue**. Prioritise inventory depth and marketing spend here.
2. **Men's category outperforms Women's in revenue** (56.26% share) despite similar unit volumes. Consider reviewing Women's pricing strategy or promoting higher-margin Women's items.
3. **Members represent 60% of transactions** but spend only marginally more than non-members. Consider enhancing the loyalty programme with tier-based incentives to widen the spend gap.
4. **Product penetration is evenly distributed** (~50% per product). This signals a healthy catalogue with no significant dead stock.
5. **The top 3-product bundle** (Grey Fashion Jacket - Women's + Teal Button Up Shirt - Men's + White Tee Shirt - Men's) appeared 352 times. A formal bundle offering could convert this organic behaviour into incremental revenue.
6. **Jeans generate the lowest segment revenue** despite healthy unit volumes. A review of the pricing or discount strategy for Jeans is recommended.
